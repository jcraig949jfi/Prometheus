# Grammata — Taxonomy & Cartography

> *From Prometheus Bound: "I invented for them the combining of letters (grammata),
> creative mother of the Muses' arts, with which to hold all things in memory."*

Grammata is the naming, mapping, and cataloging layer for the Prometheus program.
As Ignis and Arcanum produce discoveries, Grammata gives them names, defines their
properties, and maps the landscape of reasoning structures.

## Purpose

- **Taxonomy**: Build a structured vocabulary for reasoning constructs discovered
  across model scales and architectures
- **Registry**: Track which findings are validated, which are provisional, which
  are superseded (carried forward from the Vesta concept)
- **Cartography**: Map the relationships between constructs — which circuits
  co-occur, which are scale-dependent, which are architecture-specific

## Registry Gates (from Vesta)

No finding is accepted into the registry until it passes validation:

1. **Reproducibility**: Result holds across at least 2 independent runs
2. **Causal verification**: Falsification battery passes (sign-flip, noise, ortho, shuffle)
3. **Scale annotation**: Result is tagged with the model scale(s) where it was observed
4. **Cross-reference**: RPH proxy classification (PRECIPITATION_CANDIDATE / WEAK_SIGNAL / NULL)

## Relationship to Symbola and Stoicheia

- **Stoicheia** are the reasoning elements themselves — the things we discover
- **Grammata** names and catalogs them — the taxonomy
- **Symbola** represents them visually — the symbolic language for human-AI communication

Grammata is the bridge: it takes a raw Stoicheia discovery and makes it referenceable,
searchable, and connectable to other discoveries.

## Status

Planned. The registry structure will emerge organically as Ignis and Arcanum
