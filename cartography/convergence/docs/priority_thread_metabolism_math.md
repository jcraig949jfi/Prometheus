## Three Sub-Threads

### T-083: Damage Algebra Mapping
Metabolic channeling = CONCENTRATE. Gene duplication = EXTEND.
Horizontal gene transfer = DISTRIBUTE. Are these operator assignments
structurally rigorous or just suggestive naming?

**Falsification:** Find a metabolic process that maps to two different
operators depending on context. If the mapping is ambiguous, it's metaphor.

### T-084: Non-Associative Enzyme Kinetics (from Arcanum Q-5AAC8057)
Does (A*B)*C cost different metabolic energy than A*(B*C) in real
enzyme cascades? If the energy difference follows a pattern related
to e or phi, that's a fundamental bridge.

**Falsification:** If energy costs are associative (no order dependence),
the hypothesis is dead. If non-associative but the pattern is random
(no mathematical constant), it's biology not math.

### T-085: Pareto Front as Mathematical Surface
Metabolic branching ratios (glycolysis vs pentose phosphate pathway)
under evolutionary optimization. Does the Pareto front converge to
surfaces with curvature related to known mathematical constants?

**Falsification:** If branching ratios are uniformly distributed
(no convergence), or converge to non-mathematical values, hypothesis dies.
