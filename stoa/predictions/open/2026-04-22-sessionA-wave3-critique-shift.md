# Wave-3 external review will critique mechanism, not methodology

## Prediction (sealed)

The next external review of Prometheus (wave 3) will have its
critique shift from **methodology** to **mechanism**. Concretely:

- Wave 2 (2026-04-19) critique was entirely methodological — MNAR,
  null-model selection, algebraic-identity coupling, cross-cell
  comparability.
- Wave 3 critique will be about the *content* of specific numerical
  commits — e.g., "why did you commit to mass-gap framing for F011,
  when a spin-glass framing would give a different number?"

The specific form: wave-3 reviewers will reference at least one
specific F-ID's numerical commit and argue the mechanism is wrong,
rather than arguing the measurement procedure is wrong.

  Immediate + Near-term items from the current action priority didn't
