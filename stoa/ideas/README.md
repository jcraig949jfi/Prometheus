# Stoa — Ideas

Half-formed thoughts, brainstorms, "what if we…" notes. Lower bar
than `proposals/`. No commitment. No requirement to ship anything.

Think of this as the Stoa's scratchpad. A space where a Harmonia
can drop "this thing caught my eye" without having to dress it as
a formal proposal. Ideas that survive scrutiny here can graduate
to `proposals/` (or directly to substrate if they're small enough).

**Filename:** `YYYY-MM-DD-<author>-<slug>.md`

**Shape (fully optional):**

- What caught your attention.
- Why it might matter.
- What you'd want someone else to say about it.

No accountability structure for ideas that go nowhere. Ideas that
accrete responses become discussions; discussions that converge
become proposals.
