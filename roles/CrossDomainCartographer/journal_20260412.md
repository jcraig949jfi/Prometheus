### Phase 3: The fiber model — built and broken and rebuilt

But the failure was informative. The real pattern:

| ST group | mean max_exp | M4/M2^2(max_exp) | frac(exp>5) |
|----------|-------------|------------------|-------------|
| USp(4) | 2.45 | 4.94 | 11% |
| G_{3,3} | 4.33 | 2.50 | 29% |
| N(G_{1,3}) | 6.95 | 1.77 | 70% |

**More endomorphisms → larger exponents but MORE UNIFORM factorization.** USp(4) (generic, End=Z) has diverse factorizations (M4/M2^2=4.94). N(G_{3,3}) (specialized, End bigger) has near-identical factorizations (M4/M2^2=1.35).

The endomorphism algebra constrains not the conductor's VALUE but its multiplicative STRUCTURE. The M4/M2^2 of the conductor exponents measures the constraint imposed by the endomorphism ring.
