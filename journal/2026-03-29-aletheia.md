### The Primitive Basis

Mathematics may be generated from 11 structural transformations:

```
COMPOSE · MAP · EXTEND · REDUCE · LIMIT · DUALIZE
LINEARIZE · STOCHASTICIZE · SYMMETRIZE · BREAK_SYMMETRY · COMPLETE
```

COMPLETE is our discovery — found when analytic continuation broke ChatGPT's 10-primitive decomposition. Verified across 6 fields (complex analysis, topology, algebra, order theory, category theory, algebraic geometry). Categorically: a reflection functor into a full subcategory defined by a structural constraint.

### The Two-Level Architecture

The primitives operate at two levels — discovered during the decomposition test:
- **Intra-domain (nodes):** MAP and REDUCE dominate. Operations compute within a field.
- **Inter-domain (edges):** EXTEND, DUALIZE, BREAK_SYMMETRY, COMPLETE, LINEARIZE. Transformations connect fields.

Same vocabulary, two organizational levels. Search = finding typed edge chains connecting operations across domains.

### Literature Position

Novel as a unifying synthesis. Not contradicted by Lawvere (1963), Goguen-Burstall (1984), Moulines (1987), or recent categorical deep learning. Each primitive has a categorical anchor. BREAK_SYMMETRY is the least categorically formalized. COMPLETE = reflection is the sharpest refinement from the literature review.

## What It Means

Athena said it: the pieces that were disconnected now have concrete interfaces.

The flywheel has typed pipes:

```
Noesis finds composition (typed primitive sequence)
  → becomes reasoning problem
    → Forge ensemble scores at 0.734
      → RLVF selects for chains that execute vs pattern-match
        → CMA-ES evolves LoRA weights on ejection heads
          → Apollo reads geometric shift
            → shift reveals which primitives the model learned
              → feeds back to Noesis as search priority
```

Every step is typed by the 11 primitives. The ejection circuit collapses transformation sequences to terminal states. The primitives name which specific moves are being skipped. Steering vectors that crack a specific trap correspond to enabling a specific primitive the circuit was blocking.

### The Tensor Train Argument

TT completion on a sparse tensor (60 known / 1.5M possible) works here because the 11-primitive basis constrains the rank. If mathematical transformations are generated by 11 moves, the interaction tensor has rank bounded by compositions of 11, which is vastly lower than 1,714². The structural regularity verified empirically is the same regularity that makes the tensor compressible.
