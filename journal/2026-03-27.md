### The Headlines

**97 tools above 80% on unseen traps across 58 categories.** This number was ZERO
on v3. The constructive computation requirement worked — tools now calculate, not
just pattern-match.

**Top 10 tools hit 94% unseen accuracy** on 58 parametrically-varied categories.
Their epistemic honesty scores are 0.44-0.50 — well calibrated. These are the
genuine reasoning evaluators.

**Tier B jumped from 73% to 85%.** Judgment traps (ambiguity, meta-reasoning,
presupposition) are now the strongest dimension. The tools recognize when questions
are problematic, not just when answers are wrong.

**Negative gap (-2.4%).** Tools score BETTER on unseen traps than seen. Zero
overfitting. The category-only diagnostic + constructive computation approach
produces genuine generalization.
