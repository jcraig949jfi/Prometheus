2. **Topological Consistency Score** – Compute the signed Laplacian *L = D – A* where *D* is the diagonal of absolute row‑sums. A contradiction creates a cycle with odd parity of negative edges; we flag such cycles by checking the product of edge weights around each fundamental cycle (using a spanning‑tree basis). Select the top *β* % (e.g., 20 %) as parents, clone them proportionally to *a(v)*, and apply mutation (same perturbation set) to produce the next generation. Iterate for *G* = 3 generations; keep the variant with highest affinity *a\***.
