## Research Question

Comprehensive survey: who else is applying machine learning, data science, or computational search methods to LMFDB data? Not just murmurations — everything. We need to know the full landscape before claiming novelty.

## Specific Questions

1. Beyond He-Lee-Oliver-Pozdnyakov (murmurations, 2022), what ML papers use LMFDB as a data source? List all papers 2020-2026 that use LMFDB for computational experiments.

2. arXiv:2502.10360 ("Machine learning the vanishing order of rational L-functions", Feb 2025) — who are the authors, what methods did they use (PCA, LDA, neural nets?), what representation (Dirichlet coefficients, zeros, or both?), and what accuracy on vanishing order prediction?

3. Oliver (ICMS 2024) — what specifically did this work do with unsupervised learning on LMFDB? PCA on what features? Was there a searchable component or just visualization?

4. Edgar Costa's work on ML and L-functions — full bibliography. What representations did he use?

5. The "Machine Learning meets Number Theory" workshop series — are there proceedings? What topics were covered in 2023-2026 sessions?

6. Has anyone applied graph neural networks or graph learning methods to the LMFDB relationship structure (isogenies, modularity, twists)?

7. Any work on "arithmetic similarity metrics" or "L-function distance functions" — formal definitions of distance between arithmetic objects beyond raw coefficient comparison?

8. The LuCaNT conference series (LMFDB, Computation, and Number Theory) — recent proceedings, especially computational methodology papers.
