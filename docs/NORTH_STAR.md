# Prometheus — North Star

> *"We are stealing fire from the gods."*

Prometheus exists to find the reasoning manifolds and signals inside large language
models. To explore novelty in the artifacts of the waste stream. To illuminate
reasoning circuitry and look for signs of meta-cognition.

If found, these paths lead to an autonomous agent that drives novelty to the
surface, generates mathematical and scientific hypotheses, and tests them in-silico
using novel constructs that can explore complex geometries the human mind could
never comprehend.

This isn't a tractable reality today. The exploration of it is.

## The Three Priorities

These compete. They must be balanced.

### 1. GPUs Saturated 24/7
The science runs continuously. Two video cards, all VRAM utilized, always. Ignis
runs and Arcanum screenings queue and auto-rotate. When one finishes, the next
starts. This is the constraint that everything else works around.

### 2. Data Orchestration
Results flow into analysis, synthesis, and papers. The Night Watchman monitors
Ignis runs in real time. Review tools narrate findings in plain English. RPH
proxy evaluation scores survivors across scales. Arcanum needs equivalent
analysis infrastructure. The data pouring out must be organized or it drowns us.

### 3. Horizon Scanning
Constantly mine the published scientific landscape. Collate, organize, and
synthesize external knowledge into our workflows and pipelines. Forward-deployed
agents scanning the horizon, evolving our datasets, integrating new findings.
The field moves fast — we must move with it.

## Why This Is Tractable Now

- We can crack open LLMs and probe tensors directly (TransformerLens, hooks)
- THOR and similar tools break the curse of dimensionality in the waste stream
- A team of AI PhDs can write papers in 3 hours overnight
- Models will grow in complexity and size — the pipeline rotates across scales
- Mathematicians work in dimensions they can't visualize using notation they
  can compute with. So can we. That's what Symbola is for.

Not in 5 years. Right now.

## The Shadow Analogy

The model's internal state is the object. Your steering vectors and fitness
scores are the shadow on the wall. With enough shadows from enough angles,
you can infer the shape of the object. You never touch the object directly.

This is Plato's Cave, but with a twist he didn't anticipate: **you choose which
light sources to aim and from which angles.** Ignis builds the rotating platform.
