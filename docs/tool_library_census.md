## Mechanism Type Classification

The 95 concepts in the Nous dictionary are classified into 4 mechanism types:

| Type | Description | Expected Strength | Observed |
|------|------------|-------------------|----------|
| **Constraint** | Imposes hard rules or boundaries (Falsificationism, Criticality, Model Checking) | Tier A parsing | Moderate — good at contradiction detection |
| **Structure** | Defines form or arrangement (Topology, Category Theory, Compositionality) | Structural decomposition | Good on Tier B, weaker on constructive Tier A |
| **Dynamics** | Governs evolution over time (Ergodic Theory, Chaos Theory, Active Inference) | Multi-turn traces | Currently underperforming — battery is static |
| **Measure** | Quantifies properties (Maximum Entropy, Kolmogorov Complexity, Information Theory) | Scoring and ranking | Solid middle tier |

## Three-Tier Tool Hierarchy (Athena's Analysis)

| Tier | Architecture | Tools | Unseen | Honesty | Role in RLVF |
|------|-------------|-------|--------|---------|-------------|
| **Elite** | A + C | 97 | **91-92%** | **0.42-0.44** | Anchor the ensemble. Strong base signal. |
| **Middle** | D (strong) | ~80 | 55-70% | 0.10-0.20 | Diverge from elite on harder cases. Variance signal. |
| **Struggling** | D (weak) + F | ~160 | <55% | <0.10 | Asymmetric negative signals. "This looks wrong" > "This looks right". |

**For RLVF:** Weight the 97 elite tools normally. Weight middle-tier tools at family level (not individual). Weight struggling tools' negative signals higher than positive. Tool disagreement (variance penalty λ·σ(S)) is the metacognitive signal.
