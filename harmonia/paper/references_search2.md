# Literature Search 2: Tensor Networks, Math+Physics Bridges

## Tensor Train / Tensor Network Foundations
1. Oseledets. "Tensor-Train Decomposition." SIAM J. Sci. Comput. 33(5), 2011. [already cited]
2. Novikov, Trofimov, Oseledets. "Exponential Machines." arXiv:1605.03795, ICLR Workshop 2017. TT for all-order feature interactions.
