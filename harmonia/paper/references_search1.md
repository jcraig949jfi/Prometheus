# Literature Search 1: Cross-Domain Math Structure

## ML/Data-Driven Number Theory
1. He, Lee, Oliver, Pozdnyakov. "Murmurations of Elliptic Curves." arXiv:2204.10140 (2022). Exp. Math. 34(3). Data-driven oscillatory patterns in EC sorted by conductor and rank.
2. He. "Machine-Learning Mathematical Structures." arXiv:2101.06317 (2021). Comprehensive ML on algebraic geometry, representation theory, number theory survey.
3. He, Kim. "Learning Algebraic Structures: Preliminary Investigations." arXiv:1905.02263 (2019). NNs distinguish algebraic structures.
4. Davies, Velickovic et al. "Advancing Mathematics by Guiding Human Intuition with AI." Nature 600 (2021). DeepMind landmark: ML guides new math theorems.
5. Agboola, He. "Machine Learning meets Number Theory: Data Science of BSD." arXiv:1911.02008 (2019). ML + TDA on 2.5M EC, BSD invariants.
6. Lample, Charton. "Deep Learning for Symbolic Mathematics." arXiv:1912.01412, ICLR 2020. Seq2seq for symbolic integration.

## Random Matrix Theory / Montgomery-Odlyzko
7. Montgomery. "Pair Correlation of Zeros of the Zeta Function." AMS (1973). Foundational GUE conjecture for zeta zeros.
8. Odlyzko. "The 10^20-th Zero of the Riemann Zeta Function." AT&T preprint (1989). Numerical verification of GUE spacing.
9. Rudnick, Sarnak. "Zeros of Principal L-functions and Random Matrix Theory." Duke Math J. 81(2) (1996). GUE universality for all L-functions.
10. Keating, Snaith. "Random Matrix Theory and zeta(1/2+it)." Comm. Math. Phys. 214(1) (2000). RMT predicts zeta moments.

## Analytic Conductor
11. Iwaniec, Sarnak. "Perspectives on the Analytic Theory of L-Functions." GAFA Special Volume (2000). Defines the analytic conductor = our Megethos.

## LMFDB / Computational
12. Cremona. "The L-functions and Modular Forms Database Project." arXiv:1511.04289 (2015). FoCM 17. The primary data source.
13. Best, Bober, Booker, Costa, Cremona et al. "Computing Classical Modular Forms." arXiv:2002.04717 (2020). Algorithms behind LMFDB MF tables.

## Tensor Decomposition
14. Oseledets. "Tensor-Train Decomposition." SIAM J. Sci. Comput. 33(5) (2011). The TT format we use.

## Ollivier-Ricci Curvature
15. van der Hoorn et al. "Ollivier-Ricci Curvature Convergence in Random Geometric Graphs." arXiv:2008.01209 (2020). ORC converges to smooth Ricci.
16. Kamtue, Sathianwiriyakhun. "Recovering Manifold Structure Using ORC." arXiv:2410.01149 (2024). ORC identifies true manifold topology.
17. Ni, Lin, Luo, Gao. "Community Detection on Networks with Ricci Flow." Sci. Rep. 9 (2019). Discrete Ricci flow on networks.

## ML in Physics/Geometry
18. He, Yau et al. "Machine Learning in Physics and Geometry." arXiv:2303.12626 (2023). ML on Calabi-Yau, broader context.
