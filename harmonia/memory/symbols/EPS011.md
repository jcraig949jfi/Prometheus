## Definition

**F011 rank-0 residual first-gap variance deficit as conductor → ∞**, under
a specified decay ansatz. This is the frontier constant of F011 LAYER 2
(the part not explained by Duenez-HKMS excised ensemble).

The value is ansatz-dependent. The canonical triple:

| ansatz | ε₀ (%) | σ (%) | χ² | source |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| power-law `eps_0 + C · (log N)^{-α}` (α fit, under-constrained) | 31.08 | 6.19 | 19.6 | sessionB a87ea026 |
| classical `eps_0 + C / log(N)` | 22.90 | 0.78 | 20.3 | sessionB a87ea026 |
| CFMS `eps_0 + C / log²(N)` | 35.83 | 0.36 | 27.4 | sessionB a87ea026 |

**Honest range:** `ε₀₁₁ ∈ [22.90, 35.83] %` across all three fixed-form
fits. Joint α-free fit (sessionB 71ff1d47): `α = 0.49 ± 0.52`,
`eps_0 = -4.07 ± 56.08` — under-constrained; data cannot distinguish
classical from power-law. Per Pattern 25 (DRAFT): never report a point
estimate from a two-parameter fit where both are poorly constrained.

**Recommended quote when referring to F011 rank-0 residual:**
> `ε₀₁₁ = 22.90 ± 0.78 %` under classical 1/log(N) ansatz (sessionB
> a87ea026). Power-law fit gives 31% but the exponent is not
> theory-pinned.

## Derivation / show work

sessionB fit three decay forms to rank-0 conductor-window scaling data
across log₁₀(conductor) bins from 3.78 to 5.59. Observed per-decade
deficits: 57.12% (low) to 44.37% (high) — 13-point monotone drop. The
asymptote is the `eps_0` in each ansatz.

Self-audit under `NULL_BSWCD`:
- With `stratifier=torsion_bin` (Mazur 15 balanced): z_block = 4.19. DURABLE.
- With `stratifier=class_size`: degenerate (null_std=0). Do not cite.
- With `stratifier=cm_binary` (only 0.9% CM): z=0.63. Insufficient.

Ergon DHKMS closed-form test (2572d7dd): DHKMS finite-N correction
predicts the **wrong direction**. Would need 25% mean unfolding bias to
explain, which is implausible. The residual is not a reference error.

Ergon zero-projections (37158e4f): deficit varies with arithmetic
complexity (isogeny class size 1→8: var/Gaudin 1.37→0.97; sha 1→36:
1.30→1.00). NOT uniform. Concentrates in arithmetically structured
sub-populations (T4 cbe7b623: CM 1.73×, class_size=3 1.78×, cm=-27 6.66×).
